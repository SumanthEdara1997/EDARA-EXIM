How to Use

✦ Green Healthy Smoothies

Simply blend 1 teaspoon of moringa powder with a banana, spinach, yogurt, or almond milk for a quick, nutrient-filled breakfast option.

✦ Moringa Herbal Wellness Tea

Stir a spoonful into a warm cup of hot water with fresh lemon juice and organic honey. It creates a refreshing, light, clean tea tasting like green matcha.

✦ Superfood Dosa & Idli Batters

Mix 1 to 2 tablespoons directly into your dosa or idli batter. It turns breakfasts a vibrant green while adding great nutrition without changing the taste!

✦ Nutrient-Rich Roti / Chapati / Paratha

Knead moringa powder directly into whole wheat flour dough to give your rotis a massive boost of dietary iron and complete proteins.

✦ Comforting Soups, Dals & Curries

Stir easily into lentils, sambhar, or vegetable soups right before finishing. It dissolves completely, making it perfect for pickier family members.

✦ Fortified Healthy Energy Bars

Perfect for mixing into oats, dates, nuts, or home-baked treats to craft premium, health-focused daily snacks.
